Q.1: Is the website only available to UoG students?

Ans: Yes, students can only register with their UoG student ID.

Q.2: Is the price fixed?

No, the price is set my the seller. Although, we cannot influence it, you may contact the seller via email to negotiate.

Q.3: How do I obtain the book?

Once, you have an agreement with the seller you may go ahead to the book page and click the purchase option. We will send you a confirmation email. Upon confirmation, the book will be listed as sold and you may set up a time and place on campus to exchange the book for cash or another payment option.
