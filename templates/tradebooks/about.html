About Tradebooks.


Tradebooks: Who are we?

Tradebooks is a non-profit marketplace ran for studets, by students. For many years we have seen the financial difficulties students face with obtaining new books while students done with their studies simply discard their used books. Tradebooks allows for a marketplace, where University of Glasgow students can easily sell there books to fellow students in need. The free marketplace allows for enough competition to have books at a low price.

Founded in 2020, our services have hitherto helped an impressive figure of 0 (zero) students to buy or sell books, with two main objectives in mind: to aid other students in what we have struggled before and with perfection. Over 99% of our products are not able to find a buyer within a week and we have not responded to over 99% of our inquiries within a day.

Our Goals & Motivations

At first, our objective was to crate a website to sell all our devaluated or just bad used book; but after hearing about the woes of our peers, we decided to open up our website to other students as well. In fact, it even inspired us to work even harder to maintain this website for our fellow University of Glasgow students.

Tradebooks is the only website exclusively offering 100% non-functional services and is also the only website that instills false hope within its users that they might sell their books
